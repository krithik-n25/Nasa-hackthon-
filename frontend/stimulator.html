🎓 How to Play

Welcome to Farm Simulator!

Take on real-world agricultural challenges using NASA satellite data. Help farmers around the globe make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Your Mission

  • Complete 18 challenges across different difficulty levels
  • Use NASA datasets like SMAP, MODIS, GPM, and ECOSTRESS
  • Make strategic decisions to optimize farming outcomes

🌱 Earning Points

  • Each completed challenge earns you +100 points
  • Reach 500 points and a sponsor plants a real tree! 🌳
  • Track your progress in the top-right corner

🎮 Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: Perfect for beginners learning NASA data basics
  • Medium: Multi-factor challenges requiring strategic thinking
  • Hard: Complex scenarios with multiple threats and constraints

🔍 Tips

  • Use the search bar to find challenges by location or dataset
  • Filter by difficulty to match your skill level
  • Read each challenge's NASA datasets and objectives carefully

Ready to save the world's farms? Click "Launch Challenge" to begin! 🚀
