🌱 About Us – NASA Farm Navigators 🚀

We are a team of innovators passionate about bridging the gap between cutting-edge space science and down-to-earth farming practices. Our mission is to make NASA's powerful open datasets—covering climate, soil moisture, vegetation health, and more—accessible and engaging for farmers, students, and curious minds alike.


Through NASA Farm Navigators, we are creating an educational farming simulation game that blends real-world satellite imagery with interactive decision-making. Players can explore scenarios in irrigation, fertilization, crop rotation, and livestock care, all while learning how data-driven insights can shape sustainable agriculture.


Our goal is simple yet impactful:

  • 🌍 Empower communities to adopt eco-friendly practices
  • 📊 Simplify complex data into intuitive visuals
  • 🎮 Make learning fun through gameplay and storytelling
  • 🚜 Show how space technology supports life on Earth

We believe agriculture is more than farming—it's the foundation of food security and climate resilience. By combining science, creativity, and play, we're helping the next generation of farmers, educators, and innovators reimagine agriculture for a sustainable future.


✨ From space to soil, we turn NASA data into farming knowledge.
